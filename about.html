About Us

Welcome to Pizzario - The place for delicious, authentic pizzas made with love and passion!

Our Story

Pizzario started with a simple idea: to bring the authentic taste of Italy right to your doorstep. Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to crafting the perfect pizza, using only the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Meet the Owner

Zain ALi

Zain Ali is the visionary behind Pizzario. With a passion for authentic Italian cuisine, he founded Pizzario to bring the rich, traditional flavors of pizza to the community. Abdullah believes in quality, innovation, and creating a friendly environment where customers feel at home.

His journey in the food industry started from humble beginnings, but with dedication and a love for pizza, Pizzario has grown into a beloved local spot. When Abdullah isn't in the kitchen experimenting with new recipes, he's spending time with his family and enjoying a good book.

Our Values

  • Quality Ingredients: We use only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients in every pizza we make.
  • Passionate Team: Our team of chefs are passionate about pizza and committed to making every slice delicious.
  • Community First: We love our local community and strive to give back in every way possible.
